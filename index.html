About Agrimaster

Four Decades of Freshness & Reliability

Agrimaster Corporation is a Philippine-based agricultural commodities trading company with over four decades of industry experience. Founded in the 1980s and headquartered in Tondo, Manila, we have evolved from a local trading enterprise into a reputable importer and distributor of premium fresh fruits and vegetables, serving a wide range of markets across the Philippines.

Our growth is a reflection of our unwavering dedication to quality, reliability, and the long-term relationships we have built with our clients and suppliers over the years. As a company deeply rooted in the agricultural sector, we understand the importance of a stable, trustworthy supply chain — and we have built our operations around delivering exactly that.

In line with our commitment to Philippine agriculture, Agrimaster Corporation has expanded beyond trading through strategic investments in local farm operations. We currently manage 20 to 30 hectares of watermelon farms, a testament to our belief in supporting domestic food production and strengthening the country's agricultural foundation from the ground up.

At Agrimaster Corporation, we do not simply move produce from one point to another — we take pride in being a reliable partner in the nation's food supply chain, bridging the gap between farm and market with integrity, efficiency, and a standard of quality we stand behind at every step.
